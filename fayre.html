What is the May Fayre?

A collection of stalls and activities to showcase the skills and talents of the school community.

An event to bring the community together around the school and share the fun!

  • Fire Engine

  • Bake sales
  • Recycled toy sales
  • Dancing demonstrations
  • A variety of food and drinks
