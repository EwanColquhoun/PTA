May Fayre

Family fun!

Join us for our spectacular May Fayre, the biggest community-led school fundraiser of the year! This vibrant family fun day is packed with exciting activities and a fantastic variety of stalls—from plant and seed sales to face painting and dazzling dance performances. Enjoy delicious food and refreshing drinks in our outdoor seating and picnic areas while soaking up the festive atmosphere. The event draws a lively crowd, making it a day to remember! Don't miss our show-stopping silent auction, the highlight of the Fayre. Tickets are available for purchase at the main school gate, and every penny raised goes towards supporting our school. Come along and be part of the magic!

  • Police Car
