What are we?

PTA members are all volunteers and are parents and carers of pupils that attend the school. We have children in school nursery and each year group, from P1-P7. Our purpose is to advance the education and wellbeing of pupils by providing facilities not normally provided by the Council. We do this by fundraising and distributing funds raised for specific purposes. We also host events for pupils, parents and carers.

What do we fund?

Over the last couple of years we have funded improvements to the school garden, new reading books, iPads, classroom resources, paid for the Christmas panto, and we are about to install brilliant new playground equipment!
